# Hapticle: The Ultimate Sensory Fidget Playground

Turn your screen into a tactile physical canvas. **Hapticle** brings the satisfying, organic, clicky sensations of real-world mechanical fidgets directly to your fingertips. Utilizing advanced physics-driven tactile feedback and real-time procedural audio synthesis, Hapticle turns every swipe, turn, and stretch into a detailed mechanical illusion you can both feel and hear.

Whether you're looking to focus, relieve stress, or simply indulge in tactile satisfaction, Hapticle provides a premium sensory escape right on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

---

## Discover the Fidget Gallery

### 1. The Safe Dial (Rotary Lock)
Feel the heavy, oiled rotation of a safe lock. Spin it freely, feel the teeth snap into place, and hear the realistic mechanical click whirr speed up or slow down with your fingers.

### 2. The Clicky Pen
The classic ballpoint pen, perfected. Experience the distinct resistance, the deep, satisfying click of the spring-loaded latch, and the metallic recoil when released.

### 3. The Tear-Off Ticket
Pull, stretch, and tear. Feel the cardboard fibers yield perforation-by-perforation under your finger, culminating in a satisfying snap when the ticket rips off the roll.

### 4. The Magnet Orbit
Experience the invisible pull of gravity and magnetism. Drag the metal orb through magnetic fields, feel the resonance swell as you get closer to the poles, and snap it into a lock.

### 5. The Viscous Blob
Stretch and deform a viscoelastic jelly mass. Feel the fluid drag resistance scale with your finger, and witness the blob divide in two with an organic bubble-burst snap.

## Features Built for Sensation

*   **Taptic Fidelity:** Tailored specifically for Apple's Taptic Engine to deliver ultra-precise micro-vibrations.
*   **Procedural Sounds:** Real-time sound synthesis mimics casing resonance and teeth-scraping friction. No stale sound loops—the audio changes dynamically based on how fast or slow you spin.
*   **Fluid Physics:** Rigid-body physics loops drive natural inertial spins, momentum releases, and springy viscoelastic stretching.
*   **Liquid Glass Interface:** A seamless radial selector menu that fades and blooms with stagger animations.
